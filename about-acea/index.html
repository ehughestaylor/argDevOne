About ACEA



Together, we are building a better Atlantic County

The Atlantic County Economic Alliance, a private sector directed, nonprofit economic development corporation that was established to business attraction, retention, and marketing efforts in Atlantic County.

Mission

The mission of the Atlantic County Economic Alliance is to drive economic growth and cultivate economic diversity in Atlantic County through the execution of the Atlantic County Economic Development Strategy and Action Plan developed in 2015 by AngelouEconomics, a nationally recognized independent consultant from Austin, Texas.

Our Core Focus

Building a robust business retention program

Marketing competitive assets to drive industry diversification

Relationship building to attract new companies

Creating partnerships to recruit, retain and promote a qualified workforce

Our Ancillary Focus

Partnering with realtors to develop and maintain an online database of commercial and industrial properties Assisting businesses in relocation with information and site selection Creating a coordinated campaign to promote the county as a location to expand food-related businesses Collecting data and sponsoring research and reports related to trends and opportunities in the county

Strategic Goals

To raise the profile of Atlantic County as the place to do business, we are empowering regional economic development, driving industry diversification, improving delivery of economic development services, and rethinking destination attraction.

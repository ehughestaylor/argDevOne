Investment



Value

With support from Atlantic County’s top company executives, college and university presidents, and business and political leaders, Atlantic County Economic Alliance is the lead agency for business attraction and retention in the area, and the single point of entry for economic growth and development. We are proud to call Atlantic County home.

Your support is an investment in our community as a rewarding place to live, work, play, and do business. Atlantic County Economic Alliance is working to improve the county’s sustainability, resulting in improved infrastructure, an expanded tax base and job creation for the next generation.

Investment Benefits

What Does Your Investment Provide?

Improved Real
Estate Market

By attracting and retaining business, the ACEA will increase the sustainability of our county's real estate market and help raise property values. An increased ratable base will benefit our municipalities, bettering our communities. The arrival of new business also results in increased demand for professional services offered by established Atlantic County firms.

Workforce Development

New business means new jobs, and a decrease in the unemployment rate in multiple sectors. The ACEA will work with new and existing businesses to ensure workforce training is available to provide the skills required to foster business growth.

Better Quality
of Life

With enhanced business activity and a broader customer base with greater disposable income, more opportunity arises for dining, shopping, and entertainment venues.
How Your Investment Dollars Will Be Spent:

Business
Attraction

Through a comprehensive lead generation and outreach strategy, the ACEA team will bring viable new projects to the county. Activities will include recruitment trips and trade conferences.

Business
Retention

The ACEA team will continue outreach to existing employers to ensure their satisfaction with the business climate and workforce, and encourage further growth and stability.

Marketing

Working collaboratively with Choose New Jersey, the privately funded nonprofit that markets the State of New Jersey as a business destination, the ACEA will produce marketing collateral for all targeted industry sectors, as well as digital ads and social media strategy to increase traffic to the website, and create awareness of Atlantic County's economic development efforts.

Investment Levels

All Investors Receive:
- Participation in annual meeting
- Eligible for election to Board of Trustees
- Recognition on website and social media campaigns
- Recognition in relocation materials
- Listing in online directory
Angels: $100,000+
- Voting membership on the ACEA’s Board of Trustees
- Leadership of Board of Advisor Sub-committee
- Table and special recognition at the ACEA’s Annual Meeting
- Premium recognition on the ACEA website, online directory and social media
- Access to the ACEA’s economic data
- Brochure and/or company materials in all visitor and relocation packets
- No charge for sponsorship and event purchases
Partners: $50,000+
- Voting membership on the ACEA’s Board of Trustees
- Table at the ACEA’s Annual Meeting
- Recognition on the ACEA website, online directory and social media
- Access to the ACEA’s economic data
- Brochure and/or company materials in all visitor and relocation packets
- Discount on all sponsorship, event purchases and speaking engagements
Builders: $25,000+
- Voting membership on the ACEA’s Board of Trustees
- Six tickets to the ACEA’s Annual Meeting
- Recognition on the ACEA website, online directory and social media
- Access to the ACEA’s economic data
- Recognition in all visitor and relocation packets
- Discount on all sponsorship, event purchases and speaking engagements
Associates: $10,000+
- Voting membership on the ACEA’s Board of Trustees
- Four tickets to the ACEA’s Annual Meeting
- Listing on the ACEA website, online directory and social media
- Access to the ACEA’s economic data
- Recognition in all visitor and relocation packets
Community Colleague: $5,000+
- Voting membership on the ACEA’s Board of Trustees
- Two tickets to the ACEA’s Annual Meeting
- Listing on the ACEA website, online directory and social media
- Recognition in all visitor and relocation packets

***Any investor of $25,000 or more is appointed as a trustee to the ACEA’s Board of Trustees

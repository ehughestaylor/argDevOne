About ACEA

The Atlantic County Economic Alliance, a private sector directed, nonprofit economic development corporation that was established to business attraction, retention, and marketing efforts in Atlantic County.

Why Atlantic County?

    • higher education

    • quality of life

Whether you are interested in starting a business, relocating, or expanding your business, Atlantic County, New Jersey offers a wealth of opportunities among its 567 square miles. Located between lush farmlands and 14 miles of sandy shorelines, Atlantic County is within a short distance of Philadelphia, New York City and Washington, DC with easy access by car, bus, rail or air. Let us show you why Atlantic County is the best address for business.
